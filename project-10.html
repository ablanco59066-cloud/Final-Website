Fire & Frost

8.5 x 11 inches

A packaging system presents a conceptual merchandise box for a fantasy music festival featuring Wardruna, Florence & the Machine, and curated soundtrack selections. Vine-like decorative motifs reference visual elements common to the featured albums, integrating natural forms with fictionalized design to suggest a treasure chest experience. Additional packaging designs, including an elven jewelry box, tomb-shaped container, gem-inspired package, and alchemist’s ingredient satchel, employ flowing patterns inspired by Art Nouveau. Each piece encourages tactile engagement, with hidden pins or buttons reinforcing the sense of discovery and adventure. The system communicates the festival’s narrative and immersive theme through form, material, and ornamentation. ducts.
