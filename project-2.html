CookieMetric: Application

402 x 874 px screen dimension

CookieMetric is an application that helps creators understand social media algorithms by using cookie profiles to understand how they cater to certain audiences. The primary SF Compact type, and Lato subheading type, are chosen to provide a bold and professional appearance. The brand colors, bright purple and blue, signal a sense of trust and innovation. The cookie logo references the use of cookie profiles as a primary mechanic of the application.
