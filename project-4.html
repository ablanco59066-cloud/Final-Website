Subculture: What is that?

16.5 x 47.5 inches

Subculture: What is that? Is a poster that presents subcultures and how they have changed with the rise of online spaces. The poster utilizes the Delight typeface as a header to attract attention and provide a clean computer aesthetic. Roboto, used for body copy, emulates the typography utilized on Apple devices. The work deliberately simulates researching subcultures on a browser. A survey is incorporated as an interactive element for the audience. This survey is meant to determine what online subculture the audience connects with and reinforce the subject matter with an interactive activity.
