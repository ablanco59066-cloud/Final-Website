Subculture: What is that?

165 x 47.5 inches

Subculture: What is that? is a poster that presents subcultures and how they have changed with the rise of online spaces. The poster utilizes the Delight typeface as a header to attract attention and provide the work with a clean computer aesthetic. Roboto which is used for body copy to emulate the typography utilized on apple devices. The work deliberately simulates a person researching the idea of subcultures on their computer. A survey is incorporated as an interactive element for the audience. This survey is meant to determine what online subculture the audience connects with and reinforces the subject matter with an interactive activity.
