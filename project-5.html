Mellow Kitty: Zine

8.5 x 5.5 inches

This project analyzes a Hello Kitty plush through an introspective framework that maps relationships between object, environment, and material. Written and visual content organizes these relationships into a table that tracks contexts in which the object appears, and the materials associated with its construction and use. typography including Cherry Bomb One and Winky Sans establishes a playful visual tone connected to adolescence. Color and typographic scale reinforce themes of childhood nostalgia.
