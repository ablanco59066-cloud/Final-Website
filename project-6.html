CTRL: BFA Exhibition invitation

9 x 8 inches

An event invitation for the CTRL BFA Exhibition targeted to professional designers. Bright greens and blues reference early CRT monitors and highlight the feeling of futuristic optimism of the 2000s. Typography, such as the Satoshi typeface, provides a clean keyboard-like look. CTRL is placed to reference familiar forms on a Mac keyboard. The interior visuals also reference a keyboard and provide a label for each student featured in the exhibition. The bottom part of the invitation showcases the full lineup of students in picture form and features practical information including the exhibition website, time, and location. The backside of the invitation is left empty for use as a self-mailer.
