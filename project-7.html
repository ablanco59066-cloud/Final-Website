History of Tieflings

1:30 minutes

This video explains the development of the playable race known as Tieflings in Dungeon and Dragons. The video begins with the 1st edition and ends with the 5th edition showcasing how the race has changed over time. Stylistic details set the tone such as the opening medieval tavern setting background. The video transitions to more paper-based details referencing the frequent use of character sheets which are used to keep track of statistical information in the game. The overall typography choice of “blank” throughout most of the video is meant to reference the fantastical and medieval tone of the game. The early type is meant to represent the mundane feeling of daily life. The black and white shift to colorful tones is meant to display contrast as primarily a roleplaying game meant to provide a fantastic escape for players.
