Rantings of a Mad Women: Book

9 x 6 inches

The guide examines symptoms of Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder as experienced by women, combining research-based information with personal narrative. Initial sections outline the purpose of the publication and summarize commonly recommended coping strategies sourced from online materials. A compiled forum of firsthand accounts follows, presented in a diary format to emphasize individual voice and collective experience within the subculture. Multicolored pages function as a visual system, referencing the spectrum of symptoms and variability across individuals. The cover utilizes holographic material that shifts color based on light and viewing angle, reinforcing themes of variation and perception. Bold typographic contrast establishes hierarchy and clarity while maintaining a structured and professional exterior.
